LalApp

LalApp is a campus-focused platform designed to manage events, fundraising, and student participation seamlessly.

What We Do

We enable students and organizers to contribute towards events, track fundraising progress, and manage payments securely.

Payments

LalApp integrates secure payment gateways to collect contributions for college events and initiatives. Users can make payments through UPI, cards, and net banking.

Use Case

Ideal for college unions, student groups, and event organizers to collect funds, distribute rewards, and manage event participation.
